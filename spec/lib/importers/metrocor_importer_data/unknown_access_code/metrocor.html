About

MetroCor is an independent agency that has been formed to provide Amateur Radio frequency coordination for the Northern New Jersey, New York City/Long Island and Westchester County portion of the downstate New York region.

The Metropolitan Coordination Association, Inc. (MetroCor) was organized by a volunteer group of concerned Amateurs to address spectrum usage issues in one of the most densely populated areas of the country.

We've provided a number of resources here to help you.

All Coordination Request must be sent to MetroCor by US Mail

Please do not send any mail Certified it will not be picked up as it could take more than an hour to wait on line to get this. If you or anyone you know has sent something in this way please resend it by regular US mail.

If you include an email address you will get a conformation of your received correspondence.
